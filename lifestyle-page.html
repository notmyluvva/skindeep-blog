THE BEAUTY PHARMACY: MDC NEXT DOOR

BERLIN-PRENZLAUER BERG - You couldn't imagine a more suitable place for Melanie Dal Canton's small beauty empire than the area around the water tower. The idyllic district, where Berlin nightlife flourished after the fall of the Wall and where stones were always thrown on May 1st, is now a tranquil residential area for mature city dwellers.Dal Canton, who came to Berlin from Lake Constance in the 1990s, began working in Andreas Murkudis' legendary store on MÃ¼nzstrasse after the obligatory partying phase for every newcomer to Berlin. She later became managing director there. The knowledge she gained and the contacts she made in Berlin's creative bubble then offered the ideal conditions for opening her own shop on Knaackstrasse.

She wanted to change that when she opened MCD Cosmetics ten years ago. And because this plan worked immediately, cosmetic treatment rooms were added five years later. Gentle treatments are offered here today, with products that can also be bought at MDC. There is detailed advice and the prices are also mentioned, which is authentic and personable. Finally, last year MDC Next Door opened, a shop for high-quality home products such as scented candles and unusual crockery. Another treatment studio will also be inaugurated in autumn. All four MDC addresses are within a one to two minute walking distance. But that's not the only reason why the brand is so popular with its regular customers: MDC obviously hits a nerve with the times, in which everyday life is also largely aestheticized. Dal Canton's passion for beauty runs through every fiber of the company. Starting with the design of the stores with their colorful fixtures, the scent that wafts through the rooms, through the brand selection to the clothing of the employees, which looks like provided by Margiela. No detail is succinctly designed here, each item is equipped with a special design history.
